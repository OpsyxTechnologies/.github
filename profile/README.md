## Who We Are

Opsyx Technologies is a veteran-founded technology company operating at the intersection of cybersecurity, financial technology, and brand protection. We bring military-grade discipline to every vertical we enter — building systems that defend, perform, and scale.

---

## What We Do

**Cybersecurity** — Offensive and defensive security consulting, Purple Team operations, and mission assurance for federal and enterprise clients.

**Financial Technology** — Algorithmic trading platforms with multi-exchange signal analysis, automated risk management, and strategy validation.

**Brand Protection** — Digital identity verification and brand authentication solutions.

**Training** — Hands-on cybersecurity education through live instruction and community-driven learning environments.
